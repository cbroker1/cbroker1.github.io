YOS™ vs. YMS

Seamless Yard Workflows From Gate To Dock

Terminal YOS™ revolutionizes yard performance and visibility with an all-in-one platform built to scale
across all yards in your network. What are the main differences between a Yard Operating System™ and a traditional YMS?

AI Computer Vision at its Core

Instead of relying solely on manual inputs or RFID tags, Terminal YOS uses its own cameras and user handhelds to power advanced AI Computer Vision that autonomously tracks and identifies every asset, person, and event in the yard. This allows for unparalleled accuracy and real-time visibility, eliminating the errors and blind spots common in other systems.

AI-Driven Autonomous Workflows

Other YMS systems manage fragmented parts of the yard and are fundamentally reporting yard activity after it happens. Terminal YOSTM leverages AI agents to prescribe critical yard processes in real-time, delivering autonomous missions from gate to dock. This holistic approach ensures seamless operations, reduces idle time, and optimizes the entire yard process without human intervention.

Single Pane of Glass Visibility

Terminal YOS centralized visibility across all yard operations, providing a level of comprehensive visibility that other systems cannot match. Our platform integrates real-time data from all sources—including cameras, sensors, and other systems—to present a unified, intuitive view of the entire yard. This centralized dashboard allows managers to see the status of every asset and activity at a glance, enabling better decision-making and proactive problem-solving.

Configurable to All Yards

Our scalable architecture is designed to be modular and highly configurable, making Terminal YOSTM adaptable to yards of any size or complexity. Unlike one-size-fits-all solutions, our system can be customized to fit the unique layout and operational needs of any facility, from small distribution centers to large intermodal terminals. This flexibility ensures that yards can grow and evolve without outgrowing their YMS, providing a long-term solution that scales with their business.

Unlocks Value of WMS/TMS

Terminal YOSTM goes beyond simple yard management by unlocking the full value of your existing Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) and Transportation Management Systems (TMS). Our system acts as an intelligent bridge, providing real-time, accurate yard data to your WMS/TMS, which in turn optimizes scheduling, inventory, and labor allocation. By creating a unified data ecosystem, Terminal YOSTM eliminates the information silos that hinder efficiency and unlocks new levels of productivity across your supply chain.

Low Disruption, High Value

Implementing Terminal YOSTM is a low-disruption process that delivers high value quickly. Unlike complex, multi-year YMS projects, our system leverages existing infrastructure and requires minimal on-site installation. This approach minimizes operational downtime and allows yards to start realizing the benefits of automation and real-time visibility almost immediately, providing a rapid return on investment.
