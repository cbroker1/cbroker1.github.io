Terminal in the Yard

Automate and Track All Critical Workflows in the Yard

The Problem

Lost Assets, Idle Moves, Hidden Risks: The Daily Yard Reality

Trailer Misplacement

Difficulty locating trailers due to poor tracking, manual yard maps, or radio call-outs.g lines of trucks waiting for check-in due to manual processes or limited gate staff

Idle Equipment & Inefficient Moves

Yard trucks spend time searching for trailers or making non-value-added shuffles.

Poor Visibility into Capacity

Operators don’t know what spaces are available or which assets are full/empty, creating inefficiencies.

Safety Risks

Congested lanes, unclear traffic flows, and lack of monitoring increase accident risk between drivers, forklifts, and pedestrians.

The Solution

Terminal Solves Problems In the Yard, Differently

01AI-Native Accuracy vs. Hardware-Heavy Guesswork

Traditional yard solutions rely on unreliable RFID tags, GPS trackers, or manual audits that constantly fail or drift out of sync. Terminal is the first AI-native yard platform, using computer vision to deliver real-time, highly accurate visibility of every trailer, truck, and asset without the cost and complexity of extra hardware.

02Unified Platform vs. Fragmented Point Tools

Most operators stitch together multiple point solutions—yard check apps, GPS trackers, security systems—that leave data gaps and operational blind spots. Terminal unifies all yard operations from gate to dock on a single platform, giving you one source of truth across every workflow.

03Scalable Across Your Network vs. Site-by-Site Silos

Competitor systems often stop at single-yard deployments or require heavy IT investment to expand. Terminal was built to scale easily across your entire network, giving you a “single pane of glass” to manage every site, with modular deployment that requires minimal IT lift.

04Digital Bridge vs. Digital Wall

Other YMS systems operate in isolation, creating silos between the yard, warehouse, and transportation. Terminal acts as a digital bridge, deeply integrating with your existing TMS and WMS so that yard activity is synchronized with dock schedules and transportation plans, unlocking the full value of your existing systems.

05Proactive Missions vs. Basic Task Tracking

Traditional YMS tools stop at reactive trailer tracking and move assignment. Terminal goes further with AI-driven missions that anticipate your next move and optimize resource deployment, along with more advanced yard applications like damage detection, safety and reefer management.
