Terminal at the Gate

Automate Flow Through The Gate And Then Go Deep On Insights

The Problem

The Gate Is A Critical Pinch Point In Yard Operations

Truck Congestion & Queuing

Long lines of trucks waiting for check-in due to manual processes or limited gate staff

Inefficient Check-In / Paperwork

Reliance on clipboards, phone calls, or manual entry slows down throughput and introduces errors

Lack of Visibility & Scheduling

Poor communication of appointment times or lack of real-time slot management leads to bottlenecks

Security & Compliance Gaps

Difficulty balancing speed with thorough inspections, driver ID validation, and compliance checks

The Solution

Terminal Solves Problems At The Gate, Differently

01Automated with AI Vision

Other providers add AI as an afterthought. Terminal was built from the ground up as an AI-native platform, with computer vision automating the majority of gate workflows. That means higher accuracy, faster throughput, and a future-proof foundation that competitors can’t match.

02Replaces Labor and Dated Process

At the gate, Terminal upgrades manual, error-prone gate check-in and check-out processes by replacing the need for gate guards and removing legacy gate systems, manual gate arms, and paper-based clipboards for logging check-ins.

03Simple, Flexible, and Scalable Deployments

Traditional gate systems are heavy on infrastructure, complex to integrate, and slow to roll out. Terminal delivers a “Terminal in a Camera” bundle — lightweight, low IT lift, and deployable in days, not months. Any yard, regardless of size or infrastructure, can achieve rapid automation without costly hardware overhauls.

04TMS Integration that Automates

Most solutions stop at partial data syncs with your TMS. Terminal goes further by combining computer vision with deep TMS integration to automate gate check-in and check-out entirely. The result? Nearly all manual errors are eliminated, and inbound/outbound flows are seamlessly orchestrated — something competitors simply can’t deliver.
